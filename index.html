How to Play 🎬

Fashion Police meets Kahoot. On a call. With your girls.

📱
Step 01
Get on a call

FaceTime, WhatsApp, Discord — wherever. Carpet Call runs alongside your call, not instead of it.

🚪
Step 02
Open a room

Host creates a room and shares a 4-letter code. Everyone joins on their own device.

👗
Step 03
Drop the look

Host reveals an outfit. Talk about it first — drag it, defend it, clock it. Then voting opens.

💥
Step 04
The Reveal

All scores drop at once. Nobody sees anyone else's rating until everyone has voted. Chaos begins.

Phase 01 · Talk First
💬 Deliberation

The outfit drops. Everyone shares their hot take. No scores yet — just opinions flying before anyone locks in a number.

Phase 02 · Vote Blind
🔒 Voting

Everyone rates out of 10. You can't see anyone else's score until everyone has voted. No copying allowed.

Phase 03 · Reveal Together
✨ The Verdict

All scores reveal simultaneously. The screaming, the defending of terrible opinions — this is the moment.
