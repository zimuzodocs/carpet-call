The game is simple.
The arguments are not.

1
Get on a call

FaceTime, WhatsApp, Discord — wherever your girls are. Carpet Call runs alongside your call, not instead of it.

2
Open a room

Host creates a room and shares a 4-digit code. Everyone joins on their own device, anywhere in the world.

3
Drop the look

Host reveals an outfit. Talk about it first — drag it, defend it, clock it. Then the voting phase opens.

4
The reveal

All scores drop at once. No one sees anyone else's rating until everyone has voted. The chaos begins here.

Three Phases · Every Look

💬
Phase 1 — Deliberation
Talk First

The outfit drops. Everyone types their hot take. No scores yet — just opinions flying before anyone locks in a number.

🔒
Phase 2 — Voting
Vote Blind

Everyone rates out of 10. You can't see anyone else's score. You can't change your answer. No copying, no influencing.

✦
Phase 3 — The Verdict
Reveal Together

All scores reveal simultaneously. The screaming, the "WHY did you give her a 4", the defending of terrible opinions — this is the moment.
