How It Works

FOUR STEPS.
FULL CHAOS.

FaceTime, WhatsApp, Discord — wherever your girls are. Carpet Call runs alongside it.

Host creates a room and shares a 4-digit code. Everyone joins in seconds, anywhere in the world.
